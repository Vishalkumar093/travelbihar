WELCOME TO OUR BIHAR
Bodhi tree is the state emblem of Bihar. This state is known for its cultural beauty and vast history. Its construction started a small fortress but later it became the kingdom of Magdha which spread till Afghanistan. In a way, Bihar is a reflection of Indian diversity as it is home to most of the Indian tongues.
